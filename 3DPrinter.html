3D Printer Project

For the final project of my design class (MIE243), my team and I were tasked to design a 3D printer that could match the printing speed and quality of high-end 3D printers while still being affordable. Using parts that my team and I designed, I was in charge of the overall assembly of the project and particularly of the assembly of the mechanism used to move the printing bed on the z-axis.

Skills

  • • Designed base of 3D printer to contain all necessary components
  • • Used Toolbox components to implement motion support and motion transmission
  • • Researched and added attachment methods to every component
  • • Implemented mechanical mates to display components’ motion
  • • Used SolidWorks rendering to get a develop realistic visual of the design
