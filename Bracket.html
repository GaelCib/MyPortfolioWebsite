C-Bracket for Wing Support

I designed a C-bracket for the University of Toronto Aerospace Team (UTAT UAS). My design aims to create a non-permanent support structure for the drone’s tail boom and outer wing. This design was crucial to the team as when implemented, it would be the only structure linking the wings to the main drone structure.

Skills

  • • Developed a part in SolidWorks and chamfered all the edges to allow for 3D printing
  • • Combined planes and axes to create holes at the angle and position required
  • • Researched modes of attachment that would be relatively lightweight
  • • Designed a plate that allows sturdy attachment while reducing stress concentrations at the surface of the carbon fiber
